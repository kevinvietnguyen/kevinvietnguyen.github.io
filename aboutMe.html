About
Kevin

My name is Kevin Viet Nguyen and I graduated in the class of 2021 from San Jose State University with a B.S. in Computer Science. I grew up in the city of San Jose as a part of a family of four. Prior to attending San Jose State University, I attended Mount Pleasant High School and had the honor of graduating as one of the Valedictorians. In my free time, I typically like to spend my time enjoying myself, whether it be playing video games or completing work.

Coding has always been an interest as mine as problem solving as always what I enjoyed most. And after spending the more recent years of my life developing my ability to code, it seems like I was not wrong. The satisfaction of completing a project or a huge milestone has been one of the best feelings I felt. While my time in the classroom may be finished, I still expect to code and maybe develop a few personal proejcts of my own.
